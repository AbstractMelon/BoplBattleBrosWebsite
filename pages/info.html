RULES AND INFO!

Code of Conduct

  1. Play fairly within the rules (Don't try and find loopholes)
  2. Treat other members and league officials with respect
  3. Offensive language, hate speech, doxing, and other forms of harassment will not be tolerated (offensive language is allowed in the "mad-beef" channel)

Tournaments

  1. If a player leaves a team in a 2v2 tournament the team will be set to inactive with one player, another player may be added to the team to become active with a ticket
  2. In order to be eligible for finals both players need to be rostered on the team 3 weeks before finals
  3. Once a player is rostered on a team, if leave their team they are not permitted to play matches for another team until Monday 11:00 (EST)
  4. MMR gets updated after matches, but rankings update after Monday 11:00 (EST)

Regions & Participation Requirements

  1. Teams must be made of entirely one region and cannot compete in other regions
  2. If an issue of rule-breaking is present a ticket is to be made when the event occurs and the situation is to be dealt with later (continue match like normal)
  3. We recommend recording your matches to be shown as proof if any rule-breaking is to occur
  4. To contest the results of a match after it has concluded a ticket is to be made with evidence
  5. As per discord age requirements, all members MUST be over the age of 13 to participate
  6. Members must only have one account and should not play under any false names (including separate Steam accounts)
  7. Team names must be different from all other teams (first come first serve basis)
  8. A Team's default MMR is set to 100 when a team is created (If a team is disbanded and remade with the same members to avoid going below 100 they will be placed on a 2-week cooldown aka. no matches)
  9. Each account must have an email linked to it (see account setup) to participate in matches
  10. Once an account is registered you may leave teams, but you cannot make a new account (to change email make a ticket)
  11. Do not falsely sign up with someone else's discord account (your email will be banned from all matches)
  12. When creating a team make a form as if joining a team and put a new (unused) team name

Matches Organization

  1. The League will generate match-ups each week of the regular season
  2. The Matches are generated on Mondays
  3. Matches must be scheduled using Discord by Friday 24:00 (EST)
  4. Matches must be scheduled via the report-scores channel in the competitive tab
  5. A Match's score must be submitted within 24 hours of their start time
  6. All Matches must be played and scores submitted by the following Monday at 11:00 (EST)
  7. Teams will receive 4 matches each week that must be played/forfeited
  8. Both Players need to be rostered before Monday 11:00 (EST) to receive matches for the week (In 2v2 Matches)
  9. If the other team refuses to respond (DM's and server pings must be sent) a ticket must be made before Friday at 24:00 (EST) with proof of a non-response
  10. Match times must be scheduled in the "Match Times" page of this server (Both teams must submit a response with the same time)

Match Formats

  1. Once a Match Begins, it must be completed in one session
  2. A match is a best-of-3 rounds, If a team is to win the first 2 rounds, the match is officially over, and no scores should be reported for the third round
  3. A round is over once one team has reached 10 wins
  4. Matches are to be played 1v1 or 2v2 (See tournament setup)
  5. Settings must be set:
  6. Abilities: 3
  7. Hide: Nothing
  8. If a team forfeits all matches for 2 weeks they will be set to inactive and will not receive matches the following weeks (A ticket must be made to become active)

Scoring Systems

  1. Both teams must confirm scores for them to be submitted (1 team submits other team reacts with üëç)
  2. Screenshots of each round must be provided (in one message)
  3. Each message should be formatted with the following: "Team1: X - Team2: X / Round1: A-A / Round2: B-B / Round3: C-C" where x is rounds won and A, B, and C are individual round scores, this should be followed by screenshots of each round

Match Forfeits

  1. In the event that a team is 15 minutes late to a match that team auto forfeits, but the other team must provide proof of attempting to contact within DM's and pings within the server (create a tick in this instance)
  2. In the instance of a forfeit, scores should be submitted "Team1: X - Team2: X" where x is replaced with "win" for the winning team and "forfeit" for the forfeiting team (The other team still has to add a reaction)
  3. In the event that no team submits a ticket and fails to contact mods about scheduling and/or scoring a double forfeit will be submitted resulting in a double MMR loss
